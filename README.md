# Sales-Trend-Visualization
📊 Sales Trend Visualization using Python

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing historical sales data to identify meaningful trends, patterns, and customer purchasing behavior. Using Python and data visualization techniques, the project transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making.

The analysis includes monthly and yearly sales trends, category-wise and sub-category-wise performance evaluation, and the identification of key sales contributors.


Objectives

- To analyze historical sales data.
- To identify monthly and yearly sales trends.
- To compare the performance of different product categories.
- To evaluate sub-category sales contributions.
- To generate meaningful business insights using visualizations.
- To support data-driven decision-making.


Dataset Information

- Dataset Name: Superstore Sales Dataset
- Source: Kaggle
- File Format: CSV
- Total Records: 9,800
- Total Features: 18

Key Attributes

- Order Date
- Ship Date
- Segment
- Region
- Category
- Sub-Category

- Sales

Analysis Performed:

Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

-Identified monthly fluctuations in sales and highlighted peak and low-performing periods.

Year-wise Sales Analysis

-Compared annual sales performance to understand business growth patterns.

Category-wise Sales Analysis

-Evaluated sales contributions across major product categories.

Sub-Category Sales Analysis

-Identified top-performing and low-performing products.

Region-wise Analysis

-Assessed sales distribution across different geographical regions.

Segment-wise Analysis

-Examined customer segment contributions to total sales.


Key Findings

- November recorded the highest monthly sales.
- February showed the lowest sales performance.
- Sales demonstrated significant growth during the later months of the year.
- 2018 emerged as the best-performing year.
- Technology was the highest revenue-generating category.
- Phones and Chairs were the top-performing sub-categories.
- Customer demand varied considerably across product groups.


Conclusion

The Sales Trend Visualization project successfully converted raw sales data into meaningful business insights through effective data analysis and visualization techniques. The findings can help organizations improve inventory planning, optimize marketing strategies, and support informed business decisions.
